Main Characters

Ardyn Izunia is the main antagonist of Final Fantasy XV. He is the Imperial Chancellor and one of the most powerful figures in Niflheim, acting as the main political force running the empire and the one responsible for its most recent Magitek technology. His is rendered immortal due to the Starscourge and supplanted as the Crystal's chosen by Noctis' ancestor, Ardyn swore revenge against the royal line and the Crystal, adopting the name "Izunia".

Ardyn Izunia

Ardyn Izunia is Chancellor of Niflheim who introduced magitek to the imperial army and amassed political power and influence. Born Ardyn Lucis Caelum, he was once a heir to the throne of Lucis chosen by the Crystal to save the world.

Aranea Highwind

Aranea Highwind is a mercenary and captain of Niflheim's Third army Corps 86th Airborne Unit. Her loyalty towards the empire she serves wavers, and her true feelings start to show when she spends time with Prince Noctis and his friends.

Iedolas Aldercapt

Iedolas Aldercapt is leader of the forces of Niflheim, who invades the country of Lucis under the pretense of signing a peace treaty to steal the Crystal. He seeks to conquer the world to fulfill the imperial dream of uniting Eos.

Ravus Nox Fleuret

Ravus Nox Fleuret is the brother of Lunafreya Nox Fleuret, and a high commander of the Niflheim army. He has desired Lucis's downfall ever since it seemingly abandoned Tenebrae when Niflheim invaded them ten years ago.
